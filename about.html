About Kim Maddox

Kim Maddox is a highly experienced outdoor adventure guide with a passion for the outdoors and a commitment to safety. With over 8 years of experience guiding on a variety of terrain and in a variety of conditions, including glaciers, deserts, mountains, and rivers, Kim has developed the expertise and skills to make your outdoor adventure an unforgettable experience.

Experience

Outdoor Adventure Guide, Miraval Resorts (Hyatt), Catalina, AZ (Seasonal, October 2022 - February 2023)

  • Led mountain biking, hiking, and roped events as an outdoor adventure guide for Miraval Resorts.
  • Acted as a lead guide for beginner to advanced mountain biking clinics and single track trail rides.
  • Guided roped events and hikes on various trailheads in Tucson.

Glacier Guide, MICA Guides, Sutton, AK (Seasonal, Summer 2018 - 2022)

  • Guided technical trekking and ice climbing on the Matanuska Glacier via helicopter access.
  • Assessed and mitigated hazards in a wilderness environment.
  • Built ropes and anchors for ice climbing and rope access.
  • Worked remotely on a glacier for 7-9 day stints while managing a mobile glacier camp.
  • Guided NASA Jet Propulsion Labs on and off the glacier.

Crew Leader, Arizona Conservation Corps (AZCC), Tucson, AZ (Seasonal, September 2018 - February 2019)

  • Led and managed a chainsaw crew of 7 for the Arizona Conservation Corps working on the Gila Watershed Project.
  • Tasked with cutting out invasives.
