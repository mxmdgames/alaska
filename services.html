FAQ

Q: What experience is required for your trips?

A: The experience required varies depending on the trip. We pride ourselves in being able to accommodate all skill levels and we can offer beginner-friendly trips.

Q: What gear and equipment do I need to bring?

A: We will provide you with all of the necessary gear and equipment for your trip. However, we recommend that you bring your own personal gear, such as clothing and footwear i.e hiking boots, wool socks, synthetic (no cotton)

Q: What is the cancellation policy?

A: We require a 50% deposit to book your trip. The remaining balance is due 30 days before your trip. If you cancel your trip more than 30 days in advance, you will receive a full refund of your deposit. If you cancel your trip less than 30 days in advance, you will forfeit your deposit.

If you have any other questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
